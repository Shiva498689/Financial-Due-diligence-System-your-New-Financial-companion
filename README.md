# III. STRUCTURAL ECONOMIC MOATS & CAPITAL EFFICIENCY

### Operational Barriers

- **Network Effects**: AMZN's diversified business model, including AWS, creates a dense network of assets and switching costs for customers.
- **Switching Costs**: AMZN's extensive technology infrastructure and ecosystem make it difficult for customers to switch to competitors.
- **Infrastructure**: AMZN's significant investments in property and equipment, particularly in AWS, create a structural advantage.

### Geographic Concentration

- **US Concentration**: AMZN's property and equipment, net, are concentrated in the United States, with $196.0 billion, $241.6 billion, and $321.9 billion as of December 31, 2023, 2024, and 2025, respectively. This concentration poses a risk if AMZN faces difficulties in this market.

### Tax Liabilities as Headwinds

- **Tax Positions**: Both companies are subject to significant uncertainties and potential risks related to tax positions, which could have a material adverse impact on financial position, results of operations, and cash flows if tax authorities prevail in assessment of additional tax due.
